National snapshot

Denmark by the numbers

  • The Capital Region has more roasteries per km² than any other Nordic capital.
  • The average Danish roastery produces under 250 kg per week—a micro-roastery country.
  • Denmark drinks 4.5 cups of coffee per person per day—top 5 in the world.
  • Copenhagen Coffee Collective was among the first in Europe to adopt 100% transparent pricing.
